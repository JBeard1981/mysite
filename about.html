Our Mission

Our mission is to provide the people of IMVU with quality profile pictures and wall hangings at a decent price. I for one am sick and tired of seeing people shell out tens of thousands of credits for something that can be produced with a minimal amount of effort with a fraction of the price. The same policies are used in the real world. Why can we not produce a quality product for less and then offer it to the world at a good price?
