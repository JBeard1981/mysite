Here you will find a listing of various styles of picture frames. There are various typs of frames listed here the simple description will identify which is what.

  • Tall - These frames are tall frames most are designed to use a 512x256 photo size.
  • Wide - These frames are wider than they are tall most using either a profile picture ratio or a 256x512 image size.
  • Profile - These are frames that are designed to use an image the same size ratio as a profile picture.
  • Multi - These frames are designed to contain multiple pictures.
  • Word - These frames are designed with various 3d words on the frame.
