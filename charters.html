Charters

A charter is a set of rules set forth to govern the use of special items or defigne how certain things are to be done.

To put it a little more simply... If there is a role you want to take on that requires special skills such as assassination or healing this is where you find out how. Other laws are listed here such as how experience is gained and applied within the realm and how to use dice.

Dice Chater

Here in Eternal Sacrament we use a mortality based dice system. However we do not use the usual run of the mill 2, 3, 4d system that everyone has. Our system instead has two extra levels of mortality, Demigod, and God.

Demigods and gods are not openly playable as everyone would want to play a demigod or god and use the 5d or 6d roll strength to bully everyone else. Before being granted Demigod dice you must first start out with mortal Halfling or immortal dice and be active. This way we know a little about your character and yourself and prove that you are not going to abuse the power given. I can’t tell you how many times I have seen a character gain power and then use it to tear down a SL.

If you wish to upgrade your mortality dice you must first reach the upper limits of your current mortality. The basic idea is to become stronger you must work for it. There are ways around this however. Should you want to upgrade your mortality without grinding out experience you can apply for a mortality upgrade by sending a message to the forum heads.

Healers Charter

Levels

Initiate- Level one healer. Initiates are beginners and can only heal low level damage. Low level damage consists of bruises scratches cuts and sprains broken noses fall under low level damage. Initiates have healing dice of 1d20. Initiates have yet to earn their registration number.

To become an initiate level healer you must complete a mock healing and send it in to temple advisor Calaria Hawthorne leadbydisire@aol.com.

Apprentice- An apprentice is a low level healer who has earned registration. After gaining the registration you can then heal slightly higher levels of damage. Slightly higher damage may include all lesser injuries and fractured bones as well as more serious cuts and bruises. As an apprentice you can perform heals and ceremonial rituals and now have 1d30 healing dice.

For an Initiate to become an apprentice they must complete 3 successful heals the logs of which must be sent to temple advisor Calaria Hawthorne leadbydisire@aol.com.

Adept - An Adept is a medium rank healer. You can now perform heal medium rank healings these consist of broken bones cuts infections and various non life threatening damage. An adept healer can now perform resurrections and have healing dice of 1d40.

For an apprentice to become adept you must perform 6 successful heals 3 ceremonial rituals or 1 resurrection. All logs must be sent to temple advisor Calaria Hawthorne leadbydisire@aol.com.

Master - Highest level of healer. Can heal any wound perform any ceremony. Masters have healing dice of 1d50.

In order to become a master you must perform 8 successful heals, 4 ceremonial rituals, or 2 resurrections. Ceremonial rituals include blessings weddings and various other ceremonies. All logs must be sent to temple advisor Calaria Hawthorne leadbydisire@aol.com.
