Here you will find a listing of various short hand terms that are used in RP. More Terms will be added in the future as I remember them.

OOC: Out of Character. Any text or speech not concerning to the role play.
IC: In Character. Speech, text, or actions that have to do with what is happening in the role play.
Mun: Short for Mundane. Basically the person behind the computer screen.
CRP: Cross Role Play. Taking out of character or real world information and applying it to the role play. Also works in reverse. For example “I don’t like that character because the person playing it did something I don’t like” or “your character hurt my character so I hate you”.
RP: Role Play
Brackets: Brackets or parenthesis are used to surround OOC text in order to keep it separate from In Character information.
AA: Assassination Attempt. An in character attempt on your character’s life. Assassination Attempts are regulated by the forum leaders and are legal. Basically your char did something bad and now a warrant has been issued for their death.
MA: Murder Attempt. An unlawful attempt on your character’s life.
KA: Kidnaping Attempt. An unlawful attempt to take and detain your character against it’s will.
DM: Death Match. A fight to the death.
Duel: A fight between two characters either for training or because one of them is mad about something.
HA: Healing Attempt. An attempt to heal someone’s wounds.
