Contact Us

If you wish to contact me about having a photo done for your imvu account please follow this link to my profile and leave a message. In your message please include the following information :

  1. Number of people to be pictured.
  2. Will the picture be submitted to the IMVU catalog as a 3d item.
  3. Who are to receive a copy of said item.
  4. What mesh style would you like used for your item. (examples can be found in pricing section of this page.)
  5. How many pictures will be produced.
